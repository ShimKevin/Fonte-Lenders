Our History

A New Chapter in Financial Solutions

Founded in 2023, Fonte Lenders is a growing and dynamic financial service provider based in the heart of Nairobi. We started with a simple mission: to provide accessible and reliable financial solutions when they're needed most.

2023 - Present

Our Beginning

We established our headquarters in Nairobi with a focus on creating simple, transparent lending solutions. Though we don't have branches yet, our digital-first approach allows us to serve clients across Kenya.

Though we're new to the financial landscape, our commitment to excellence has quickly established us as one of the best lending partners in the region. We're proud of our rapid growth and the trust we've built with our clients.
